Movie Webpage Project

Context

In this project, we had to use our knowledge of HTML (Hyper Text Markup Language) and CSS (Cascading Style Sheets) to make a movie. This was done on replit with a partner. My partner was Billy and the name of our movie was "Summer".

Content

We created a movie using the genre of horror. Summer is something many students await because they can somewhat relax, but don't want it to end. It was the start of summer and Bob felt really tired. Once he touched his bed, he fainted. When he woke up, he looked at his calendar and realized summer was over. He couldn't believe what he saw and remembered that he had summer assignments, so he fainted again. After he woke up, he rechecked his calendar and saw it was the wrong one.

Reflection

Challenges

A challenge we faced was figuring out what to write for the story. We also struggled to adjust the spacing between elements. For example, it took us a while to figure out how to move the border of the title (Summer) closer to it.

Takeaways

  • Communication is important
  • Planning is really helpful
