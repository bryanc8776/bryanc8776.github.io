Freedom Project: Accounting

Context

The "Freedom Project" for SEP10 is a year-long project all about making a website that informs the viewer of the current and future innovations in the topic of my choosing. For my project, I chose the topic of Accounting. I used HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, and Github. I also chose to independently study A-Frame in order to help me make by website.

Reflection

Challenges

A challenge I faced was researching for the existing technology in accounting which includes softwares like apps and hardwares like computers. After some struggle and searching up different words related to technology and accounting, I found multiple apps, laptops, desktops, and gadgets. Another challenge I faced was learning my tool and building a potential model for my future invention because I didn't manage my time correctly so I had to focus on building my website and then build the A-Frame model.

Takeaways

  • It is important to plan and manage your time correctly
  • Using specific and words related to a topic can help find more information
  • It is important to plan what you want your website to look like first on mobile and then on bigger sceens
  • Something can be produced with imagination and creativity

Next Step

A next step is to put more effort into making something like my website because since I didn't manage my time correctly it didn't come out like I would've wanted it to. Another next step is to learn more on my own because I didn't really try a lot of different A-Frame features like I wanted to which prevented me from making a better, more complex model.
